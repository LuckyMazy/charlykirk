About Charlie Kirk

Charles James Kirk (born October 14, 1993) is an American conservative political commentator, radio host, author, and activist. He is best known as the founder and president of Turning Point USA (TPUSA), a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting free-market and constitutionalist principles on college campuses across the United States.

Born in Prospect Heights, Illinois, Kirk grew up in a suburb of Chicago. He is a prominent figure in conservative media and politics and has been a vocal supporter of traditional American values, limited government, and free enterprise.

Turning Point USA

Turning Point USA (TPUSA) is a non-profit organization founded by Kirk in 2012. Its mission is to identify, educate, train, and organize students to promote the principles of free markets, limited government, and American exceptionalism.

TPUSA operates chapters at thousands of high schools and colleges across the country, providing students with resources, speakers, and events to engage with conservative ideas. The organization hosts AmericaFest, one of the largest annual conservative student conferences in the nation.
